Get Started

Reporting protocol

1. Signup on the Site

Signup on the website with your school crdentials to help identify what lost or found items belong to whom.

2. Report your Item

Use the website report form to state where you lost your item or found an item.
Give an accurate description and use images if possible.

3. Send/receive at security desk

Deliver the found item to a security desk once a
potential owner reaches out. If you are an owner pick up
you item from a security desk.
